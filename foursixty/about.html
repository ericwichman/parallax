About Us

Founded in September 2012, Foursixty's mission is to help musicians achieve their goals of music industry success and to help expand both their local and global fan bases. Foursixty enables musicians to build and manage their own mobile apps, enhancing the way musicians connect with their fans and acquire new followers.

Any musician can sign up for Foursixty and anyone can discover, download, interact with, and share apps. Foursixty is a privately held company and is headquartered in Toronto, Canada.

Why We Created Foursixty

With Foursixty, we want to give musicians a tool that is in line with today’s most popular technologies, and provide a solution that enables them to obtain full control of their content and social media platforms. Moreover, Foursixty will provide musicians with something that is uniquely their own. Musicians looking to generate traction in the music industry, or among friends and fans, will be able to cohesively and formally brand themselves with a more complete distribution channel and promotional tool.

With this technology, fans will always have the most up-to-date information in the palm of their hands, and artists will be able to ensure that their newest content reaches their fans with utmost efficiency. Perhaps the biggest thing that Foursixty will accomplish is that any musician can create an app anywhere. What we’re really doing is giving musicians the opportunity to create and promote their own apps the way that they would a website or a blog. With Foursixty, the ability to reach a limitless number of fans becomes a reality.
